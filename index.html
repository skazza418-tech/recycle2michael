At Recycle 2 Michael, giving back is part of the mission. A portion of every sale — along with all suitable furniture — is donated directly to the local Dogs Trust.

Your support helps provide food, medical care, warm bedding, and a safe environment for dogs waiting for their forever homes. Every collection you book makes a real difference.
